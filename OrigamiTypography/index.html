ORIGAMI TYPOGRAPHY

Zhiyu Bai © 2016

I was inspired by a technique of paper folding called 折り紙 (origami) from Japan. As I found origami and typography possess some common features - they are both shaping the space with with shapes and proportion. So I design a set of origami typography for display and apply it in a kinetic webpage poster.

Handiworks like origami possess a nostalgic spirit of humanism and love. Dating back to 1680, this traditional decoration is used in ceremonies and entertainment activities in the oriental world. On the other hand, its pattern and construction illustrates the logical aethetics of mathematics. It is a combination of two poles of knowledge and the intuition, a cohension of the mystery of nature and the intellegence of human.

Here, we celebrate the minimalistic and dynamic attributes of origami through typography on digital media. Its revival on screen-based interface, I believe, provides inspirations for the visual as well as the tangible interactions in terms of design and programming.
